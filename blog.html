Setting up the Minecraft Realm

July 5, 2026

Spent the weekend tinkering with the dueling addon and world for the bedrock realm. The goal is to have a functionable, centralized interaction system implemented to the realm within a month. There have been a lot of advertising efforts on my end and trying to form new friendships has been tough, but I am finding the Minecraft community to be very chill and mostly abiding by our peaceful server rules. However, we do have an issue currently with players throwing down custom builds into the world and then immediately leaving. Potential fixes involve removng the custom bases starter pack from the starting inventory via command blocks or custom scripting. Still not exactly sure how I'm going to approach this but I will provide updates as they happen.
